Just So You Know

The Church of Christ Hymns (COC HYMNS) App was designed to provide brethren with access to a wide range of hymns and spiritual songs suitable for all services of the church and special occasions. It provides access to a collection of hymns for personal and non-commercial use only. For commercial uses, see the terms and conditions of the composers or publishers of the hymns or songs.

The hymns included in this app are believed to be in the public domain, and every effort has been made to ensure their accuracy and relevance. However, I cannot guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or error-free of the content provided in this App. I do not claim any ownership or copyright over the hymns or spiritual songs, which belong to their respective authors, composers, and publishers.

The opinions expressed in the hymns are those of their respective authors and do not necessarily reflect the views of the developer. Furthermore, this app is intended to supplement rather than replace traditional hymn books used for all services of the church and special occasions.

The hymns, lyrics, and other content provided in this app are subject to change without notice. The app and its developer do not endorse any particular interpretation or use of the hymns, and are not responsible for any use or misuse of the content provided by the app.

By using this app, users agree to release the app and its developer from any liability or claims arising or in any way connected with the user's use of the app and its content.

If you have concerns with your songs being used in this app or you believe permission was not sought before usage, kindly send an email. Remember to proof ownership of the song or hymn. Thank you.

Appreciations

Special thanks to the authors of the hymns and Spiritual songs who have allowed their songs to be used freely and to many brethren who offered suggestions and encouragement throughout this work. God Bless you all.

Dedication

This App is dedicated to all Christians throughout the world who understand the words of our Lord that says "speaking to one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord" - Ephesians 5:19 (NKJV)
